Uploading an image

Images are used on the WI website to help cut heavy text into more legible blocks. Images also bring attention to the page and allow for longer retention rates.

Steps:

  1. Go onto the page where you would like to insert the image.
  2. In the body, click on where you want to insert the image.
  3. Scroll down to Upload an Image and select Choose File.
  4. Select the image you want to upload and click Open.
  5. Click on Upload.
  6. Change the image size by clicking on the Style drop down menu.
  7. Click Insert.
  8. Add appropriate alternative text.
  9. Click save.
Things to note
  • If your image is more than 10 MB it will not upload
  • Images must be between 10x10 and 3000x3000 pixels
  • Make sure to enter proper alternative text for images as this is what screen readers read
