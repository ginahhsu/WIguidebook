Waterloo International Guidebook

While supporting the University of Waterloo (UW) in becoming one of the most internationalized universities in Canada, Waterloo International (WI) tackles the hurdle of engagement and awareness to the international public eye. In order to do so, the communications team at WI has been tasked with creating content that showcases the internationalization of UW, providing resources for students, staff, and faculty, as well as connecting with other universities to expand our horizons.

Under the umbrella of communications at WI, tasks can be set up into a few different categories: web content, photo-graphics, and illustration-graphics. This guidebook has been created in order to assist staff with the delivery of these projects with easy to follow along tutorials.
