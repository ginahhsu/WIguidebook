Google Sites

Creating a site

Adding content

Currently, the only part of the WI website hosted on Google sites is the International Agreements Map. The site is very easy to use and very similar to many content management systems (CMS).

Creating a site:

  1. On the WI email, open new Google Sites.
  2. At the top, under Start a new site, select a template.
  3. Edit your site.
  4. Click on publish at the top right to publish your changes.
Things to note
  • Make sure you are signed into the wicomm@uwaterloo.ca email before you get started.
  • For the most updated information on publishing, be sure to check on Sites Help.

Adding content:

  1. Go to the right side of the page to view the different content options.
  2. Choose which content you would like to insert.
  3. Choose where you would like the content to go.
    If you put the content in the wrong location, you can drag and drop it to where you would like.
  4. Hit publish to save and publish your new content.
Things to note
  • For more detail on how to add or edit specific content, visit the sites help page.
  • The current WI agreement map is a link on the page. Depending on the software, you may choose to embed it instead.
